About Me

Hello, my name is Christopher J Torres Santiago and I am originally from Puerto Rico. I come from a rather large and close-knit family that likes to party, eat and have fun. I graduated from the University of Puerto Rico with a degree in Criminal Justice and Forensics. Join the army in 2016 as an Information Technology Specialist. For the past 7 years I have served with pride my nation, in which I had the joy of visiting places I never imagined to see visited. I love to have fun with family and friends in the beach and being relaxed where I can drink, dance and have fun. I am passionate about sports and I like to visit new stadiums whenever I can. I am always instructing in new areas of knowledge because I think life is about that. I love to know new cultures and countries with my wife because there is nothing more beautiful than the memory of doing and seeing new or different things with the person that you love the most.
